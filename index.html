About The Venue

Amplifying Raw Talent into Sonic Reality

Located in the heart of Hull, O’Rileys is more than just a music venue; it is a cornerstone of the city’s independent creative scene. Known for its raw, authentic atmosphere and professional-grade production, O’Rileys serves as a high-octane hub for live music, community events, and alternative subcultures.

Whether it’s a national touring band, a local grassroots showcase, or a televised sporting event, O’Rileys delivers an unrivaled experience for performers and fans alike.
